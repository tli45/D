How Does the Ollie Work?

Understand the physics and motion behind this iconic move.

Dynamics Breakdown

The Ollie is not just a jumpâ€”it's a carefully timed combination of force, friction, and motion. Below is a breakdown of the key stages of an Ollie:

  • Pop: The back foot slams the tail of the board against the ground, generating upward force.
  • Slide: The front foot slides forward, leveling out the board mid-air.
  • Level Out: Both feet align the board for a smooth landing.

Key Forces Involved

  • Ffoot: Force applied by the back foot on the tail.
  • Ntail: Normal force on the tail from the ground.
  • Nboard: Upward force from the board reacting to the front foot slide.
  • Wskater, Wboard: Weight forces acting downward.
